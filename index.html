HindSight: A DeafSpace Project

A device inteded to allow Deaf people to react to stimuli outside their peripheral vision. See what we've done so far...

What is HindSight?

HindSight is a wearable piece of technology with embedded microphones and vibrating buzzers whose intention is to identify any spikes in volume outside the peripheral vision of the user and give them a sense of direction and urgency in order to react to the stimulus.
